Nigerian Buns Recipe

So far, things seem to be turning out good.

Here is our recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g All purpose flour
  • 3tbsp of butter
  • 1tbs baking powder
  • 100 - 150ml water
  • 1tbs of salt
  • 2 eggs (to make it richer)
  • 2tbsp of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 100g of sugar or honey whichever you prefer
  • 100ml milk (optional). I like my buns very rich 😋

Instructions

  1. Add all the dry ingredients with the butter in a bowl and mix to form crumble.
  2. Add the egg, milk, and water and mix to form a thick stretchy batter, then add water to the flour in bits till you achieve the desired consistency. Do not overwork the batter.
  3. Place a shallow pan on medium heat, add vegetable oil and allow to heat up till hot.
  4. By dropping in a small batter, test the oil for readiness. If the batter floats to the top, then it's ready.
  5. Carefully drop the batter in the hot oil in batches but don't overcrowd the oil.
  6. Reduce the heat and fry the buns till golden brown and well done.
  7. Repeat this process until you have exhausted the whole batter mix.
  8. Enjoy!

Note: Always reduce the heat each time you are frying a new set then turn the heat back up to for even cooking. This way the buns doesn't turn brown quickly without cooking properly. Do this and thank me later.
